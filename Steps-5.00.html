Suggestions 5

Suggestions

1. Cut costs : Source for cheaper prices from suppliers without compromising on quality. Carry out a market  Cost analysis over the discounts being given and see if there is any  benefit being derived from the additional cost.

2. Increase online sales:  Focus on closing loss making branches and increase online sales in those geographic regions to increase sales.

3. Drop Products : Drop Products that are no longer selling  or making losses.

4. Price Review: For the products being sold at a price less than the cost, review/increase the prices to reflect the increased cost / make a profit where possible.
